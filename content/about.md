## Who We Are

Morristown-Hamblen EMS is the primary emergency medical services provider for Morristown and Hamblen County, Tennessee. We are dedicated to delivering the highest standard of pre-hospital emergency care to every member of our community.

## Our Service Area

We proudly serve the City of Morristown and all of Hamblen County, responding to medical emergencies, trauma calls, and interfacility transports around the clock.

## Our Commitment

Every call we receive is someone's emergency. Our crews respond with urgency, skill, and compassion — because when you need us most, we will be there.
