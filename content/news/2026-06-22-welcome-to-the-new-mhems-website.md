Morristown-Hamblen EMS is pleased to announce the launch of our new official website.

We are excited to bring our community a modern, easy-to-navigate resource for information about our service, our team, and what we do every day to serve Morristown and Hamblen County.

In the coming weeks, we look forward to sharing news and updates about our agency, highlighting the dedicated men and women who make up our crew, and providing information on opportunities to join our team.

We invite you to explore the site and check back often as we continue to grow our online presence. If you have questions or would like to get in touch, visit our Contact page — we'd love to hear from you.
